How to add this signature to Gmail
  1. Click "Copy Signature HTML" above
  2. Open Gmail and click the gear icon (top right)
  3. Click "See all settings"
  4. Scroll down to the "Signature" section
  5. Click "Create new" and give it a name
  6. Click inside the signature editor, press Ctrl+A (or Cmd+A on Mac) then Ctrl+V (Cmd+V)
  7. Scroll down and click "Save Changes"
  8. Set it as your default signature for new emails and replies
